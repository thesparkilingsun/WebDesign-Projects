CoVid-19

What is Cornoa virus?

Coronaviruses (CoV) are a large family of viruses that cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS-CoV) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS-CoV).

How does it spreads

Co-Vid 19 is a special strain of corona virus family.The virus is thought to spread mainly from person-to-person. Between people who are in close contact with one another (within about 6 feet).
Through respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs or sneezes. These droplets can land in the mouths or noses of people who are nearby or possibly be inhaled into the lungs.

Symptoms

Cough, like normal flu

Cold, excessive sneezing

Fever,like normal flu

Pnemonia, Extreme Case

CoVid-19 and World

Virus Spread around the world

WHO characterizes COVID-19 as a pandemic, Coronavirus Cases as mentioned by worldometers.com are a staggering total of 135,112 which include Deaths-4,990 and Recovered-70,405

Cure and Vaccine

There is currently no vaccine to prevent coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus.
As of now there is no vaccine available but scientist are working to develop one at NIAID(National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases).

Precautions

Cover nose and mouth when coughing and sneezing with tissue or flexed elbow

wear facemask if you go outside, if infected it prevent from spreading to others.

Seek medical attention if you have cough, cold or fever for more than 2days

Avoid going outside, especially to places which have confirmed cases
